Our Services

RESIDENTIAL

Speed service provide a complete range of residential locksmith services and solutions for residents of the Treasure Valley. We install, repair, and replace locks, safes, and gates.

COMMMERCIAL

Whether you’re a business owner responsible for a retail store, warehouse, single office, or entire building,Speed service provides high-quality commercial services and security services.

KEYS & LOCKOUTS

Lock yourself out of your mailbox? Need to rekey your new home, a new door lock, or duplicate keys? You can count on us!

ELECTRONIC KEYPADS

Never worry about keys again! We can install keyless entry systems that electronically lock your doors and keep you safe. With a variety of finishes and style options, you can pair style and protection.

AUTOMOTIVE

Speed service is a fully licensed auto locksmith. We’ll arrive quickly to retrieve keys locked in the vehicle. Whether you have a car or motorcycle, you can count on us.

SAFE LOCK

Your safe is home to your most important assets whether that’s paperwork or products. But what happens if something goes wrong? Speed service provides maintenance, combination changes and installs electric keypads for home, office, depository, and other commercial safes.
